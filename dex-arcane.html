About Dex Arcane Builds

Dex Arcane builds focus on poisoning, bleeding, and occult damage. These builds excel at applying status effects while maintaining fast attack speeds.

Why Choose Dex Arcane?

  • Poison & Bleed: Apply status effects quickly.
  • Mobility: High Dexterity for swift combat.
  • Item Discovery: High Arcane boosts loot.
  • Occult Scaling: Unique weapons scale with Arcane.

Example Build: Poison Blade

Build Overview

The Poison Blade specializes in applying poison and bleed effects through rapid attacks.

Stat Distribution (Level 150)

  • Vigor: 40 — Decent survivability.
  • Mind: 15 — Minimal FP needs.
  • Endurance: 25 — Standard equipment load.
  • Strength: 10 — Base stat.
  • Dexterity: 80 — Maximize attack speed.
  • Intelligence: 10 — Base stat.
  • Faith: 10 — Base stat.
  • Arcane: 60 — Boost poison, bleed, and item discovery.

Weapons & Gear

Right Hand: Nagakiba or Uchigatana with occult affinity.

Left Hand: Serpentbone Shield for poison resistance.

Armor: Light equipment for mobility.

Talismans:

  • Dragoncrest Greatshield Talisman (physical defense)
  • Ritual Pot Talisman (reduced item use)
  • Goldmask's Order Seal (buff after pattern)

Strategy

  • Apply Poison: Use poison weapons for consistent damage.
  • Bleeding: Alternate to blood weapons for status buildup.
  • Mobility: Rely on quick rolls and fast attacks.

Pros and Cons

Pros:

  • Powerful status effects.
  • Fast attack speed.
  • Great item discovery.

Cons:

  • Low survivability.
  • Status effects can be cured.
  • Requires positioning skill.
