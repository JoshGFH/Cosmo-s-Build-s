About Pure Strength Builds

Pure Strength builds focus entirely on maximizing physical damage through high Strength investment. These builds are simple, powerful, and excel in close-quarters combat.

Perfect for players who prefer straightforward melee gameplay without the complexity of magic or hybrid stats.

Why Choose Pure Strength?

  • High Damage: Weapons deal massive physical damage.
  • Simple Stat Investment: Focus all points on Strength and supporting stats.
  • Tanky: Heavy armor provides excellent defense.
  • Reliable: No FP or spell management.

Example Build: Strength Warrior

Build Overview

The Strength Warrior is a no-nonsense build that crushes enemies with raw power.

Stat Distribution (Level 150)

  • Vigor: 50 — High health pool.
  • Mind: 10 — Base stat.
  • Endurance: 30 — Heavy armor and weapons.
  • Strength: 80 — Maxed for scaling.
  • Dexterity: 10 — Base stat.
  • Intelligence: 10 — Base stat.
  • Faith: 10 — Base stat.
  • Arcane: 10 — Base stat.

Weapons & Gear

Right Hand: Greatsword or Claymore for high damage.

Left Hand: Shield for defense.

Armor: Full heavy armor set.

Talismans:

  • Great-Jar's Arsenal (boosts attack power)
  • Starscourge Heirloom (increases dexterity)
  • Dragoncrest Shield Talisman (boosts defense)
  • Green Turtle Talisman (stamina recovery)

Combat Strategy

Boss Fights: Aggressive melee attacks, use weapon arts.

PvE Exploration: Crush enemies with powerful swings.

Multiplayer: Tank and deal damage.

Best Weapons for Pure Strength

Two-Handed Weapons

  • Greatsword: High damage, slow swings.
  • Claymore: Balanced damage and speed.
  • Battle Axe: Fast attacks.
  • Warhammer: Blunt damage.

Pros and Cons

Pros:

  • Extremely high damage.
  • Simple to play.
  • Strong defense with heavy armor.

Cons:

  • No magic or healing.
  • Slow movement.
  • Vulnerable to ranged attacks.
