About the Wretch Build

The Wretch Build is the ultimate masochistic challenge in Elden Ring. You start with level 1 stats and never level up, never upgrade your gear, and rely purely on skill, positioning, and environmental factors to defeat enemies.

This build is named after the "Wretch" class, which starts with the lowest possible stats. It's not about being powerful—it's about proving that even the weakest Tarnished can conquer the Lands Between.

Why Play Wretch?

  • Ultimate Challenge: The hardest difficulty possible.
  • Skill Building: Forces perfect positioning and timing.
  • Story Appreciation: Experience the game as intended for the weakest.
  • Community Respect: Few players complete this challenge.

The Build: True Wretch

Build Overview

You literally start as a Wretch class and never spend a single rune on leveling. No gear upgrades, no smithing stones, no spirit ashes. Just you, your starting gear, and your wits.

Stat Distribution (Level 1 - FOREVER)

  • Vigor: 10 — Fragile as glass.
  • Mind: 11 — Minimal FP.
  • Endurance: 10 — Low stamina.
  • Strength: 10 — Weak attacks.
  • Dexterity: 13 — Slightly better.
  • Intelligence: 9 — Can't cast spells.
  • Faith: 8 — Can't use incantations.
  • Arcane: 12 — For discovery.

Weapons & Gear

Right Hand: Club (starting weapon) - never upgrade it.

Left Hand: Shield (starting) - never upgrade it.

Armor: Starting Wretch armor - never change it.

Talismans: None. You find talismans but don't equip them (that's cheating).

Combat Strategy

Boss Fights: Parry, dodge, use terrain, fall damage, poison, bleed. Never direct fight.

PvE Exploration: Sneak, ambush, environmental kills, fall damage on enemies.

Multiplayer: Don't do invasions - that's not fair to others.

Wretch Build Variations

1. Pure Wretch (No Items)

Rules: No consumables, no spirit ashes, no crafting.

The most challenging version - rely only on your starting gear.

2. Environmental Wretch

Rules: Can use environmental hazards and fall damage.

More forgiving - use the world against your enemies.

3. Discovery Wretch

Rules: Can use items but never equip better gear.

Focus on exploration and item discovery rather than combat.

Essential Wretch Strategies

Combat Techniques

  • Perfect Parry: Learn to parry every attack.
  • Fall Damage: Drop enemies from heights.
  • Poison/Bleed: Use environmental poisons and bleeding weapons you find.
  • Stealth: Avoid direct confrontation.
  • Terrain: Use cliffs, pits, and traps.

Famous Wretch Victories

  • Malenia (dodge her waterfowl, use scarlet rot)
  • Margit (parry, fall damage)
  • Godrick (environmental kills)
  • Radahn (festival star crash)

Pros and Cons

Pros:

  • Immense satisfaction from impossible victories.
  • Forces you to learn every mechanic.
  • Deep appreciation for game design.
  • Bragging rights in the community.

Cons:

  • Extremely frustrating and time-consuming.
  • Some bosses are nearly impossible.
  • Limited to specific strategies.
  • Not recommended for casual play.
