About Rivers of Blood Samurai

The Rivers of Blood katana is one of the most powerful weapons in Elden Ring, scaling with both Dexterity and Arcane. This meta build maximizes the weapon's potential while maintaining excellent mobility.

Why This Build is Meta

  • Insane Damage: Rivers of Blood deals massive bleed damage with charged attacks.
  • High Mobility: Samurai class provides excellent Dex scaling from the start.
  • Bleed Synergy: Arcane boosts bleed buildup and item discovery.
  • Versatile: Works in PvE and PvP with proper positioning.

Build Overview

Stat Distribution (Level 150)

  • Vigor: 40 — Moderate survivability.
  • Mind: 15 — Base stat.
  • Endurance: 25 — Medium equipment load.
  • Strength: 10 — Base stat.
  • Dexterity: 80 — Maximize weapon scaling and attack speed.
  • Intelligence: 10 — Base stat.
  • Faith: 10 — Base stat.
  • Arcane: 60 — Boost bleed damage and item discovery.

Weapons & Gear

Right Hand: Rivers of Blood katana (requires Dex 12, Arc 18).

Left Hand: Buckler or parrying dagger.

Armor: Light samurai armor for mobility.

Talismans:

  • Millicent's Prosthesis (boosts dexterity scaling)
  • Rotten Winged Sword Insignia (attack power buff)
  • Claw Talisman (critical damage boost)
  • Dragoncrest Greatshield Talisman (defense)

Combat Strategy

  • Charged Attacks: Use fully charged heavy attacks for massive bleed buildup.
  • Positioning: Stay mobile and use the environment for hit-and-run tactics.
  • Bleed Stacking: Multiple hits build bleed quickly for devastating procs.
  • Critical Hits: Backstabs and ripostes deal insane damage.

Getting Started

Early Game

  1. Start as Samurai class for optimal Dex scaling.
  2. Level Dexterity to 12 and Arcane to 18 to wield Rivers of Blood.
  3. Farm for runes in early areas to level up quickly.
  4. Find the Uchigatana as a temporary weapon.

Mid Game

  1. Obtain Rivers of Blood from the Bloody Finger Okina in Mt. Gelmir.
  2. Upgrade the weapon to +10 with Somber Smithing Stones.
  3. Collect talismans that boost dexterity and critical damage.
  4. Practice charged attack timing for maximum bleed buildup.

End Game

  1. Max out Dexterity and Arcane for optimal scaling.
  2. Equip the best bleed-boosting talismans.
  3. Master the weapon's attack patterns and recovery frames.
  4. Use the build against tough bosses and in PvP.

Pros and Cons

Pros:

  • Extremely high damage output with bleed procs.
  • Excellent mobility and positioning options.
  • Strong in both PvE and PvP.
  • Rewarding skill ceiling.

Cons:

  • Requires precise timing for charged attacks.
  • Vulnerable during attack recovery.
  • Bleed can be cured by certain enemies/items.
  • Lower damage against bleed-immune enemies.
