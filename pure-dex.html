About Pure Dexterity Builds

Pure Dexterity builds maximize attack speed, critical damage, and evasion. Perfect for players who value skill and precision over raw power.

Why Choose Pure Dexterity?

  • Attack Speed: Fastest attacks in the game.
  • Critical Damage: High backstab and riposte damage.
  • Mobility: Superior rolling and evasion.
  • Skill-Based: Rewards player positioning and timing.

Example Build: Assassin

Build Overview

The Assassin relies on speed, positioning, and critical strikes.

Stat Distribution (Level 150)

  • Vigor: 40 — Moderate health.
  • Mind: 10 — Base stat.
  • Endurance: 30 — Medium equipment load.
  • Strength: 10 — Base stat.
  • Dexterity: 99 — Maximize everything.
  • Intelligence: 10 — Base stat.
  • Faith: 10 — Base stat.
  • Arcane: 10 — Base stat.

Weapons & Gear

Right Hand: Katana or Hand Axe for critical damage.

Left Hand: Buckler for backstabs.

Armor: Light clothing for maximum mobility.

Talismans:

  • Claw Talisman (boosts critical damage)
  • Dragoncrest Greatshield Talisman (defense)
  • Rotten Winged Sword Insignia (buffs attack with shield)

Combat Strategy

  • Backstabs: Position behind enemies for critical hits.
  • Speed: Use attack chains and quick evasion.
  • Parry/Riposte: Master parrying for burst damage.

Popular Pure Dex Builds

1. Katana Master

Focus: 99 DEX | Role: Swift blademaster

Uses Uchigatana or Nagakiba for aesthetic and power.

2. Dual Wield Dancer

Focus: 99 DEX | Role: High-speed attacker

Dual wields daggers or scimitars for insane attack speed.

3. Bow Master

Focus: 99 DEX | Role: Ranged specialist

Focuses on bow damage and arrow application.

Pros and Cons

Pros:

  • Highest attack speed.
  • Superior critical damage.
  • Maximum mobility and evasion.

Cons:

  • Very low survivability.
  • Requires precise positioning.
  • Lower damage per hit than Strength.
