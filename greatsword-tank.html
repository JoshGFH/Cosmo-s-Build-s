About Greatsword Tank

This meta Strength build focuses on colossal greatswords and heavy armor, creating an unstoppable tank that can shrug off most attacks while dealing devastating damage.

Why This Build is Meta

  • Insane Damage: Colossal weapons deal massive physical damage.
  • Tankiness: Heavy armor provides excellent defense and poise.
  • Simple: Straightforward Strength investment with minimal complexity.
  • Reliable: Works against any enemy type without special requirements.

Build Overview

Stat Distribution (Level 150)

  • Vigor: 60 — Maximum health for tanking.
  • Mind: 10 — Base stat.
  • Endurance: 40 — Heavy armor and weapons.
  • Strength: 80 — Maximize weapon scaling.
  • Dexterity: 10 — Base stat.
  • Intelligence: 10 — Base stat.
  • Faith: 10 — Base stat.
  • Arcane: 10 — Base stat.

Weapons & Gear

Right Hand: Greatsword or Zweihander (colossal weapons).

Left Hand: Large shield for defense.

Armor: Full heavy armor set (Crucible, Banished Knight, etc.).

Talismans:

  • Great-Jar's Arsenal (attack power boost)
  • Dragoncrest Greatshield Talisman (defense)
  • Starscourge Heirloom (dexterity boost for requirements)
  • Green Turtle Talisman (stamina recovery)

Combat Strategy

  • Heavy Attacks: Use charged attacks for maximum damage.
  • Shield Defense: Block and punish with devastating counterattacks.
  • Poise: Heavy armor prevents staggering from most attacks.
  • Positioning: Use the environment and weapon reach to control space.

Pros and Cons

Pros:

  • Massive damage output.
  • Excellent survivability.
  • Simple to play.
  • Works against any enemy.

Cons:

  • Slow movement and attacks.
  • Vulnerable to ranged attacks.
  • High stamina consumption.
  • Limited mobility.
