About Arcane Faith Builds

Arcane Faith builds combine holy incantations with occult status effects. These builds offer unique scaling and powerful combination attacks.

Why Choose Arcane Faith?

  • Holy Magic: Faith incantations with Arcane scaling.
  • Status Effects: Apply poison and bleed through spells.
  • Item Discovery: High Arcane for loot boosting.
  • Utility: Healing, buffs, and debuffs.

Example Build: Holy Apostate

Build Overview

The Holy Apostate combines Faith incantations with Arcane status effects.

Stat Distribution (Level 150)

  • Vigor: 45 — Moderate health.
  • Mind: 35 — FP for spellcasting.
  • Endurance: 20 — Medium equipment.
  • Strength: 10 — Base stat.
  • Dexterity: 10 — Base stat.
  • Intelligence: 10 — Base stat.
  • Faith: 60 — Incantation damage.
  • Arcane: 60 — Status effects and item discovery.

Weapons & Gear

Right Hand: Fingerprint Stone Shield or Occult Mace.

Left Hand: Erdtree Seal or Giant-Crusher Seal.

Armor: Medium faith-scaling armor.

Talismans:

  • Radagon Icon (incantation scaling)
  • Dragoncrest Greatshield (defense)
  • Ritual Pot Talisman (FP reduction)

Incantations

  • Holy Ground: Healing and damage.
  • Flame, Grant Me Strength: Buff incantation.
  • Dragoncrest Greatshield: Defense buff.

Pros and Cons

Pros:

  • Unique status scaling.

Cons:

  • Lower damage than pure spellcasters.

  • Narrow weapon selection.
