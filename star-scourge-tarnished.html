About the Star Scourge Tarnished

This build is my take on the Star Scourge — a strength and gravity focused Tarnished inspired by General Radahn. Built around the Starscourge Greatswords and battlefield control sorceries, it focuses on pulling enemies in, overwhelming them with raw force, and ending fights with explosive, cinematic pressure. It's not about speed. It's about presence.

Why Choose Star Scourge Tarnished?

  • Battlefield Control: Gravity sorceries pull enemies into your effective range.
  • Cinematic Combat: Overwhelming presence with powerful strikes and spells.
  • Radahn Inspiration: Channel the might of the Star Scourge himself.
  • Strength Focus: Raw power combined with magical control.

Example Build: Radahn's Chosen

Build Overview

A strength-focused build with gravity sorceries that commands the battlefield like General Radahn himself.

Stat Distribution (Level 150)

  • Vigor: 50 — High health to withstand prolonged fights.
  • Mind: 25 — FP for gravity sorceries.
  • Endurance: 30 — Stamina for heavy attacks and spells.
  • Strength: 75 — Power for Starscourge Greatsword.
  • Dexterity: 15 — Base stat.
  • Intelligence: 30 — Gravity sorceries.
  • Faith: 15 — Base stat.
  • Arcane: 10 — Base stat.

Weapons & Gear

Right Hand: Starscourge Greatsword (upgraded) - The signature weapon.

Left Hand: Shield or another Starscourge Greatsword for dual wielding.

Armor: Radahn's armor set or heavy armor for the aesthetic.

Talismans:

  • Starscourge Heirloom (strength boost)
  • Great-Jar's Arsenal (attack power)
  • Dragoncrest Shield Talisman (defense)
  • Green Turtle Talisman (stamina recovery)

Combat Strategy

Boss Fights: Use gravity sorceries to pull bosses close, then overwhelm with heavy strikes.

PvE Exploration: Control crowds with gravitational pulls and finish with powerful swings.

Multiplayer: Command the battlefield and protect your team.

Key Sorceries for Star Scourge Tarnished

1. Gravitas

Effect: Pulls enemies toward you | Use: Close distance for melee attacks

The bread and butter of battlefield control.

2. Collapsing Stars

Effect: Falling star projectiles | Use: Area denial and damage

Channel Radahn's gravitational power.

3. Stars of Ruin

Effect: Massive gravitational explosion | Use: Boss finishers

The ultimate expression of overwhelming force.

Pros and Cons

Pros:

  • Excellent battlefield control with gravity magic.
  • Cinematic and powerful combat style.
  • Perfect for Radahn cosplay enthusiasts.
  • Combines melee power with magical utility.

Cons:

  • Requires good positioning for gravity spells.
  • Heavy weapons can be slow against fast enemies.
  • FP management is important for spell usage.
  • Less effective in tight spaces.
