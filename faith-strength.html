About Faith Strength Builds

Faith Strength builds prioritize Faith for powerful incantations, with enough Strength to wield heavy weapons and seals. These builds excel in magical damage and utility, using Strength for close-range backup.

Ideal for players who enjoy spellcasting but want the option for melee combat when spells aren't enough.

Why Choose Faith Strength?

  • Magical Dominance: High Faith unlocks devastating incantations and buffs.
  • Versatile Damage: Switch between holy magic and physical attacks.
  • Utility Spells: Access to healing, buffs, and crowd control.
  • Defensive Options: Spells like Barrier of Gold for protection.

Example Build: Holy Warrior

Build Overview

The Holy Warrior focuses on Faith for incantation power, with Strength for weapon requirements and occasional melee damage.

Stat Distribution (Level 150)

  • Vigor: 45 — Decent survivability.
  • Mind: 30 — High FP for frequent spellcasting.
  • Endurance: 20 — Enough for medium armor.
  • Strength: 25 — Meet weapon requirements.
  • Dexterity: 10 — Base stat.
  • Intelligence: 10 — Base stat.
  • Faith: 60 — Maximize incantation scaling.
  • Arcane: 10 — Base stat.

Weapons & Gear

Right Hand: Sacred Relic Sword or Zweihander for incantation scaling.

Left Hand: Giant-Crusher Seal for maximum Faith scaling.

Armor: Crucible Axe set or similar for incantation boosts.

Talismans:

  • Radagon Icon (boosts incantation damage)
  • Godfrey Icon (increases charged attacks)
  • Dragoncrest Greatshield Talisman (boosts physical defense)
  • Green Turtle Talisman (increases stamina recovery)

Incantations & Spells

  • Lightning Spear: Powerful projectile attack.
  • Flame, Grant Me Strength: Attack buff.
  • Barrier of Gold: Defensive barrier.
  • Heal: Self-healing.
  • Black Flame: High damage incantation.

Combat Strategy

Boss Fights: Use incantations from range, switch to melee for close encounters.

PvE Exploration: Spam spells for crowd control.

Multiplayer: Provide ranged support and buffs.

Level 150

Level 200+

Best Weapons for Faith Strength

Incantation Scaling Weapons

  • Sacred Relic Sword: Scales with Faith, good for charged attacks.
  • Zweihander: Heavy weapon with incantation scaling.
  • Morning Star: Blunt weapon for close-range.

Seals

  • Giant-Crusher Seal: Best for Faith scaling.
  • Erdtree Seal: Balanced scaling.

Pros and Cons

Pros:

  • High magical damage output.
  • Versatile with spells and melee.
  • Strong in ranged combat.

Cons:

  • Lower physical damage.
  • FP management required.
  • Vulnerable in close quarters without spells.
