About Torch Builds

Torch builds turn the humble torch - an item meant for lighting dark areas - into a devastating weapon. By infusing torches with special effects and using them as your primary weapon, you create a build that's equal parts practical and hilarious.

This build shines (pun intended) in dark areas and against enemies weak to fire, while providing constant illumination for your journey.

Why Choose Torch Combat?

  • Always Lit: Never fight in the dark again.
  • Fire Damage: Effective against many enemies.
  • Utility: Lights braziers and reveals secrets.
  • Meme Value: The ultimate "practical" weapon.

Example Build: Flame Illuminator

Build Overview

A strength-based build that turns torches into flaming weapons of mass destruction.

Stat Distribution (Level 150)

  • Vigor: 45 — Good survivability.
  • Mind: 20 — FP for spells.
  • Endurance: 30 — Stamina for attacks.
  • Strength: 70 — Torch damage scaling.
  • Dexterity: 15 — Base stat.
  • Intelligence: 20 — Flame spells.
  • Faith: 20 — Fire incantations.
  • Arcane: 10 — Base stat.

Weapons & Gear

Right Hand: Torch (infused with special effects).

Left Hand: Shield or another torch for dual wielding.

Armor: Medium armor for balance.

Talismans:

  • Great-Jar's Arsenal (boosts torch damage)
  • Flame Guardian (fire damage boost)
  • Green Turtle Talisman (stamina)
  • Dragoncrest Shield Talisman (defense)

Combat Strategy

Boss Fights: Use fire infusions, cast flame spells between attacks.

PvE Exploration: Light the way and burn enemies.

Multiplayer: Unexpected fire damage catches opponents off guard.

Torch Build Variations

1. Flame Bearer

Focus: 70 STR/20 FTH | Style: Holy fire

Uses flame incantations with torch attacks.

2. Pyromancer

Focus: 70 STR/20 INT | Style: Magical fire

Combines torch with flame sorceries.

3. Dual Flame

Focus: 60 STR/60 DEX | Style: Dual wielding

Wields two torches for maximum fire damage.

Torch Weapon Guide

Torch Types

  • Standard Torch: Basic fire damage, lights the way.
  • Beast-Repellent Torch: Extra holy damage.
  • Sentry's Torch: Lightning damage option.
  • Ghostflame Torch: Spirit damage.

Torch Infusions

  • Blood: Bleed buildup
  • Poison: Poison buildup
    • Magic: Extra damage
  • Holy: Holy damage

Torch Tips

  • Always keep a torch equipped for lighting.
  • Use torch attacks to light braziers from range.
  • Torches work great in dark dungeons.
  • The fire damage helps against undead enemies.

Pros and Cons

Pros:

  • Constant light source - never get lost in dark areas.
  • Effective fire damage against many enemies.
  • Can light torches and braziers from range.
  • Hilarious to watch and play.

Cons:

  • Low damage compared to real weapons.
  • Limited range and attack speed.
  • Some players find it too gimmicky.
  • Weak against fire-resistant enemies.
