About Dex Intelligence Builds

Dex Intelligence builds blend swift weapon attacks with powerful sorceries. These builds excel in ranged combat and mobility while maintaining solid melee damage.

Why Choose Dex Intelligence?

  • Mobility: High Dexterity enables fast rolls and quick attacks.
  • Magical Power: Intelligence grants access to devastating sorceries.
  • Versatility: Switch between melee and ranged combat seamlessly.
  • Crowd Control: Use spells to manage multiple enemies efficiently.

Example Build: Swift Mage

Build Overview

The Swift Mage combines quick, precise attacks with ranged sorcery.

Stat Distribution (Level 150)

  • Vigor: 40 — Moderate survivability.
  • Mind: 35 — High FP for frequent spellcasting.
  • Endurance: 25 — Medium armor and weapons.
  • Strength: 10 — Base stat.
  • Dexterity: 70 — Maximize attack speed and evasion.
  • Intelligence: 60 — Maximize sorcery damage.
  • Faith: 10 — Base stat.
  • Arcane: 10 — Base stat.

Weapons & Gear

Right Hand: Katana or Dagger with magic affinity.

Left Hand: Astrologer's Staff for Intelligence scaling.

Armor: Light equipment for fast rolling.

Talismans:

  • Carian Filigreed Crest (boosts sorcery damage)
  • Dragoncrest Greatshield Talisman (physical defense)
  • Ritual Pot Talisman (reduces FP consumption)

Sorceries

  • Glintstone Pebble: Fast, low-cost projectile.
  • Adula's Moonblade: AOE sorcery.
  • Shard Spiral: Crowd control spell.

Pros and Cons

Pros:

  • High mobility and fast casting.
  • Versatile in combat.
  • Strong ranged damage.

Cons:

  • Lower physical durability than Strength builds.
  • Less melee damage than pure Dex builds.
  • FP management required.
