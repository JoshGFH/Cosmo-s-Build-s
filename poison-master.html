About Poison Master

This meta Arcane build focuses on maximizing poison damage and status effect buildup. Using occult weapons and high Arcane scaling, this build can melt enemies with toxin over time.

Why This Build is Meta

  • Insane DoT: Poison deals massive damage over time.
  • Item Discovery: Maximum loot drop rates.
  • Occult Scaling: Unique weapons that scale with Arcane.
  • Area Control: Poison mist and cloud effects.

Build Overview

Stat Distribution (Level 150)

  • Vigor: 50 — High health for survivability.
  • Mind: 15 — Base stat.
  • Endurance: 30 — Medium equipment.
  • Strength: 20 — Weapon requirements.
  • Dexterity: 20 — Weapon requirements.
  • Intelligence: 10 — Base stat.
  • Faith: 10 — Base stat.
  • Arcane: 99 — Maximize poison damage and item discovery.

Weapons & Gear

Right Hand: Poison Dagger or Serpentbone Blade.

Left Hand: Poison Shield or additional poison weapon.

Armor: Medium armor with poison resistance.

Talismans:

  • Dragoncrest Greatshield Talisman (defense)
  • Ritual Pot Talisman (item use reduction)
  • Green Turtle Talisman (stamina recovery)
  • Immunizing Horn Charm (poison resistance)

Combat Strategy

  • Poison Application: Use weapons and items to apply poison.
  • Area Denial: Poison Mist and Poison Cloud for crowd control.
  • Stacking: Multiple poison sources for faster buildup.
  • Positioning: Keep distance while poisoning enemies.

Getting Started

Early Game

  1. Start as any class and focus on Arcane early.
  2. Find poison weapons and items.
  3. Level Arcane to unlock better poison effects.
  4. Farm poison-resistant enemies for items.

Mid Game

  1. Obtain occult weapons from Nokron and other areas.
  2. Learn poison incantations and sorceries.
  3. Collect poison-boosting talismans.
  4. Practice poison stacking techniques.

End Game

  1. Max out Arcane for optimal scaling.
  2. Equip the best poison and defense talismans.
  3. Use against poison-vulnerable enemies.
  4. Master area denial with poison clouds.

Pros and Cons

Pros:

  • Massive damage over time.
  • Excellent item discovery.
  • Strong crowd control.
  • Works against groups of enemies.

Cons:

  • Poison can be cured.
  • Slow damage buildup.
  • Ineffective against poison-immune enemies.
  • Requires careful positioning.
