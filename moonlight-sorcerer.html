About Moonlight Sorcerer

The Moonlight Greatsword is one of the most powerful Intelligence-scaling weapons in Elden Ring. This meta build maximizes lunar sorcery damage while maintaining excellent spellcasting capabilities.

Why This Build is Meta

  • Insane Damage: Moonlight Greatsword deals massive Intelligence-scaled damage.
  • Lunar Magic: Access to powerful moon sorceries like Lunar Blade.
  • Versatile: Combines melee and ranged combat effectively.
  • High Scaling: Excellent Intelligence scaling on both weapon and spells.

Build Overview

Stat Distribution (Level 150)

  • Vigor: 40 — Moderate survivability.
  • Mind: 50 — High FP for spellcasting.
  • Endurance: 20 — Light equipment.
  • Strength: 10 — Base stat.
  • Dexterity: 10 — Base stat.
  • Intelligence: 80 — Maximize sorcery and weapon scaling.
  • Faith: 10 — Base stat.
  • Arcane: 10 — Base stat.

Weapons & Gear

Right Hand: Moonlight Greatsword (requires Int 38).

Left Hand: Carian Regal Scepter or Lusat's Glintstone Staff.

Armor: Light robe or cloth for mobility.

Talismans:

  • Carian Filigreed Crest (sorcery boost)
  • Ritual Pot Talisman (FP reduction)
  • Godfrey Icon (charged attack boost)
  • Intelligence-boosting talisman

Sorceries

  • Lunar Blade: Powerful charged attack sorcery.
  • Adula's Moonblade: AOE lunar damage.
  • Moonlight Barrage: Rapid projectile attacks.
  • Glintstone Comet: Massive damage potential.

Combat Strategy

  • Charged Attacks: Use weapon skill for massive damage.
  • Spell Casting: Mix sorceries with melee attacks.
  • Positioning: Keep distance for spell casting, close for weapon attacks.
  • FP Management: Use FP-restoring items and careful spell usage.

Getting Started

Early Game

  1. Start as Prisoner or Astrologer for Intelligence scaling.
  2. Level Intelligence to 38 to wield Moonlight Greatsword.
  3. Learn basic glintstone sorceries.
  4. Farm runes to level up quickly.

Mid Game

  1. Obtain Moonlight Greatsword from Ranni's questline.
  2. Upgrade the weapon and learn lunar sorceries.
  3. Collect Intelligence-boosting talismans.
  4. Practice spell-weapon combos.

End Game

  1. Max out Intelligence for optimal scaling.
  2. Equip the best sorcery-boosting talismans.
  3. Master the weapon's attack patterns.
  4. Use against magic-vulnerable enemies.

Pros and Cons

Pros:

  • Extremely high damage output.
  • Versatile combat options.
  • Excellent against magic-weak enemies.
  • Strong in both PvE and PvP.

Cons:

  • High FP consumption.
  • Vulnerable during spell casting.
  • Lower damage against magic-resistant enemies.
  • Requires careful FP management.
