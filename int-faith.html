About Int Faith Builds

Int Faith builds combine sorceries with incantations, offering massive magical versatility. These builds provide access to both sets of spells for unmatched utility.

Why Choose Int Faith?

  • Spell Variety: Access to sorceries and incantations.
  • Holy & Arcane Power: Multiple damage types.
  • Utility: Healing, buffs, and damage spells.
  • Flexibility: Adapt to any situation with spells.

Example Build: Holy Mage

Build Overview

The Holy Mage combines Intelligence and Faith for comprehensive magical coverage.

Stat Distribution (Level 150)

  • Vigor: 45 — Moderate health.
  • Mind: 50 — High FP for two spell types.
  • Endurance: 15 — Light equipment.
  • Strength: 10 — Base stat.
  • Dexterity: 10 — Base stat.
  • Intelligence: 60 — Sorcery damage.
  • Faith: 60 — Incantation damage.
  • Arcane: 10 — Base stat.

Weapons & Gear

Right Hand: Carian Regal Scepter or Holy Seal.

Left Hand: Astrologer's Staff and Seal.

Armor: Robe or cloth.

Talismans:

  • Carian Filigreed Crest (sorcery boost)
  • Radagon Icon (incantation boost)
  • Ritual Pot Talisman (FP reduction)

Spells

  • Sorceries: Glintstone, Moonblade, Comet.
  • Incantations: Holy Ground, Golden Order's Seal, Heal.

Pros:

  • Maximum spell versatility.

Cons:

  • High FP consumption.
