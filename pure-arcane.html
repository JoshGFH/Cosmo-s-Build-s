About Pure Arcane Builds

Pure Arcane builds focus entirely on status effects and occult scaling. These specialized builds excel at applying poison, bleed, and other ailments, plus maximizing loot discovery.

Why Choose Pure Arcane?

  • Status Mastery: Apply multiple ailments simultaneously.
  • Item Discovery: Maximum loot drop rates.
  • Occult Scaling: Unique weapons scale with Arcane.
  • Niche Playstyle: Stand out from other builds.

Example Build: Plague Lord

Build Overview

The Plague Lord specializes in applying multiple status effects and poisoning entire enemy groups.

Stat Distribution (Level 150)

  • Vigor: 50 — High health for survivability.
  • Mind: 15 — Base stat.
  • Endurance: 30 — Medium to heavy equipment.
  • Strength: 20 — Weapon requirements.
  • Dexterity: 20 — Weapon requirements.
  • Intelligence: 10 — Base stat.
  • Faith: 10 — Base stat.
  • Arcane: 99 — Maximize status effects and loot.

Weapons & Gear

Right Hand: Occult Scythe or Occult Mace.

Left Hand: Occult Shield.

Armor: Heavy occult-scaling armor.

Talismans:

  • Dragoncrest Greatshield Talisman (physical defense)
  • Ritual Pot Talisman (item use reduction)
  • Godfrey Icon (charged attack boost)

Weapon Strategy

  • Poison Application: Use poison weapons for consistent buildup.
  • Bleed Buildup: Alternate between poison and blood weapons.
  • Loot Farming: Maximize item discovery with 99 Arcane.
