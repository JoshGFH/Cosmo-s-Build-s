About Pure Intelligence Builds

Pure Intelligence builds focus entirely on sorceries, offering the highest magical damage output. These builds are powerful ranged specialists.

Why Choose Pure Intelligence?

  • Maximum Sorcery Damage: Unmatched spell power.
  • Spell Variety: Access to all glintstone sorceries.
  • Ranged Combat: Attack from safe distances.
  • Crowd Control: Multiple AOE spells.

Example Build: Full Sorcerer

Build Overview

The Full Sorcerer dedicates all resources to Intelligence and spell power.

Stat Distribution (Level 150)

  • Vigor: 40 — Basic survivability.
  • Mind: 50 — High FP for extended spellcasting.
  • Endurance: 10 — Base stat.
  • Strength: 10 — Base stat.
  • Dexterity: 10 — Base stat.
  • Intelligence: 99 — Maximize sorcery damage.
  • Faith: 10 — Base stat.
  • Arcane: 10 — Base stat.

Weapons & Gear

Right Hand: Carian Regal Scepter or Lusat's Glintstone Staff.

Left Hand: Academy Glintstone Staff.

Armor: Minimal, light robes.

Talismans:

  • Carian Filigreed Crest (sorcery boost)
  • Ritual Pot Talisman (FP reduction)
  • Godfrey Icon (charged attack boost)
  • Intelligence-boosting Talisman

Sorceries

  • Glintstone Pebble: Fast casting.
  • Moonblade: Heavy hitter.
  • Comet: Rapid attacks.
  • Adula's Moonblade: AOE damage.
  • Shard Spiral: Crowd control.
