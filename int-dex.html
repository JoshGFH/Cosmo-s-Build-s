About Int Dexterity Builds

Int Dexterity builds emphasize sorcery with Dexterity as a secondary stat. These builds excel in spell slinging with melee backup.

Why Choose Int Dexterity?

  • Magic Focus: High Intelligence for powerful sorceries.
  • Evasion: Dexterity enables quick dodges between spells.
  • Flexibility: Balance magicand melee combat.
  • Range: Dexterity scales with bows for ranged support.

Example Build: Spellblade

Build Overview

The Spellblade focuses on Intelligence with Dexterity for fast melee attacks.

Stat Distribution (Level 150)

  • Vigor: 40 — Moderate survivability.
  • Mind: 40 — High FP for spellcasting.
  • Endurance: 20 — Light equipment.
  • Strength: 10 — Base stat.
  • Dexterity: 50 — Fast weapon scaling.
  • Intelligence: 70 — Maximize sorcery damage.
  • Faith: 10 — Base stat.
  • Arcane: 10 — Base stat.

Weapons & Gear

Right Hand: Moonveil Katana or Rapier with magic affinity.

Left Hand: Astrologer's Staff or Royal Staff.

Armor: Light robe or cloth.

Talismans:

  • Carian Filigreed Crest (sorcery boost)
  • Ritual Pot Talisman (FP reduction)
  • Godfrey Icon (charged attack boost)

Sorceries

  • Moonblade: Charged projectile.
  • Adula's Moonblade: AOE damage.
  • Glintstone Comet: Rapid casting.
