About Arcane Dexterity Builds

Arcane Dexterity builds combine swift attacks with powerful status effects and item discovery. These specialized builds excel at applying poison, bleed, and other ailments.

Why Choose Arcane Dexterity?

  • Status Effects: Rapid poison and bleed buildup.
  • Item Discovery: Higher Arcane boots loot drop rates.
  • Occult Weapons: Access to unique occult scaling weapons.
  • Mobility: Dexterity for fast attacks and evasion.

Example Build: Poison Dancer

Build Overview

The Poison Dancer specializes in rapid poison application through quick attacks.

Stat Distribution (Level 150)

  • Vigor: 40 — Moderate health.
  • Mind: 15 — Minimal FP needs.
  • Endurance: 25 — Medium equipment.
  • Strength: 10 — Base stat.
  • Dexterity: 80 — Attack speed and evasion.
  • Intelligence: 10 — Base stat.
  • Faith: 10 — Base stat.
  • Arcane: 60 — Status effects and loot.

Weapons & Gear

Right Hand: Nagakiba or Scimitar with occult affinity.

Left Hand: Serpentbone Shield.

Armor: Light equipment.

Talismans:

  • Dragoncrest Greatshield Talisman (defense)
  • Dragoncrest Greatshield Talisman (physical defense)
  • Ritual Pot Talisman (reduced item use)

Strategy

  • Poison Application: Use poison weapons for consistent buildup.
  • Attack Speed: Rapid hits maximize status buildup.
  • Loot Farming: High Arcane boosts item discovery for farming.
