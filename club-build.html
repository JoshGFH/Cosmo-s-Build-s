About Club Builds

Club builds embrace primitive weaponry, using clubs, hammers, and other blunt instruments to smash enemies into paste. This build focuses on raw power and the satisfying crunch of impact damage, while looking absolutely ridiculous.

Perfect for players who want to roleplay as a caveman or just enjoy the meme value of using "primitive" weapons in a high fantasy setting.

Why Choose Club Combat?

  • High Damage: Blunt weapons hit hard.
  • Stagger Potential: Great for interrupting attacks.
  • Meme Appeal: Looks completely out of place.
  • Simple Fun: Smash everything in sight.

Example Build: Caveman Crusher

Build Overview

A strength-focused build that turns primitive weapons into devastating tools of destruction.

Stat Distribution (Level 150)

  • Vigor: 50 — High health for tanking.
  • Mind: 10 — Base stat.
  • Endurance: 35 — Heavy weapons need stamina.
  • Strength: 80 — Maxed for club scaling.
  • Dexterity: 10 — Base stat.
  • Intelligence: 9 — Base stat.
  • Faith: 9 — Base stat.
  • Arcane: 12 — Discovery.

Weapons & Gear

Right Hand: Club, Mace, or Morning Star (upgraded).

Left Hand: Shield for defense.

Armor: Heavy armor to fit the caveman aesthetic.

Talismans:

  • Great-Jar's Arsenal (damage boost)
  • Starscourge Heirloom (strength boost)
  • Dragoncrest Shield Talisman (defense)
  • Green Turtle Talisman (stamina)

Combat Strategy

Boss Fights: Heavy swings, stagger bosses, use weapon arts.

PvE Exploration: Crush enemies with powerful strikes.

Multiplayer: Tank and smash invaders.

Club Build Variations

1. Primitive Warrior

Focus: 80+ STR | Style: Pure strength

Uses basic clubs and maces for maximum smash potential.

2. Spiked Clubber

Focus: 70 STR/20 ARC | Style: Bleed focused

Uses spiked clubs for bleed buildup and occult scaling.

3. Hammer Lord

Focus: 80+ STR | Style: Heavy hitter

Specializes in massive hammers and warhammers.

Best Club Weapons

Club Options

  • Club: Basic wooden club, starting weapon.
  • Spiked Club: Bleed damage, occult scaling.
  • Mace: Blunt damage, good for staggering.
  • Morning Star: Bleed and physical damage.
  • Warhammer: Massive damage, slow but powerful.

Club Build Tips

  • Focus on staggering enemies to create openings.
  • Use heavy attacks for maximum impact.
  • Position yourself for charged attacks.
  • Heavy armor helps with the caveman aesthetic.

Pros and Cons

Pros:

  • Extremely high damage potential.
  • Great stagger and interrupt capability.
  • Simple and satisfying combat.
  • Perfect for roleplaying primitive characters.

Cons:

  • Slow attack speed compared to lighter weapons.
  • Low range and mobility.
  • Vulnerable to ranged attacks.
  • Some find the aesthetic too silly.
