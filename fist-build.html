About Fist Builds

Fist builds embrace the monk lifestyle, using only your bare hands (and maybe some brass knuckles) to defeat enemies. This build is all about close-quarters combat, perfect positioning, and turning your fists into lethal weapons.

While it may seem silly at first, fist builds can be surprisingly effective and satisfying once you master the timing and reach of unarmed attacks.

Why Choose Fist Fighting?

  • Unique Combat: Different feel from weapon combat.
  • High Mobility: Fast attacks and dodges.
  • Skill Intensive: Requires perfect timing.
  • Meme Potential: Looks ridiculous but hits hard.

Example Build: Bare Knuckle Brawler

Build Overview

The ultimate fist fighter, combining high strength with dexterity for devastating unarmed attacks.

Stat Distribution (Level 150)

  • Vigor: 40 — Decent survivability.
  • Mind: 15 — Some FP for buffs.
  • Endurance: 35 — High stamina for combos.
  • Strength: 60 — Fist damage scaling.
  • Dexterity: 60 — Attack speed and accuracy.
  • Intelligence: 9 — Base stat.
  • Faith: 9 — Base stat.
  • Arcane: 12 — Discovery.

Weapons & Gear

Right Hand: Caestus or Grafted Dragon (upgraded fists).

Left Hand: Nothing - keep it free for combos.

Armor: Light armor for mobility (Bloodhound set works great).

Talismans:

  • Great-Jar's Arsenal (boosts unarmed attacks)
  • Green Turtle Talisman (stamina recovery)
  • Assassin's Crimson Dagger (bleed buildup)
  • Starscourge Heirloom (dexterity boost)

Combat Strategy

Boss Fights: Close distance, use quick combos, dodge-roll through attacks.

PvE Exploration: Rush down enemies, use grab attacks.

Multiplayer: Fast, aggressive playstyle.

Popular Fist Build Variations

1. Bloodhound Fist Fighter

Focus: 60 STR/60 DEX | Style: Fast and bloody

Uses Bloodhound Claws for bleed-focused unarmed combat.

2. Dragon Fist Monk

Focus: 80 STR | Style: Powerful grappler

Grafted Dragon for devastating grab attacks and fire damage.

3. Brass Knuckle Boxer

Focus: 50 STR/50 DEX | Style: Balanced fighter

Caestus for classic boxing-style combat.

Best Fist Weapons

Unarmed Options

  • Caestus: Basic brass knuckles, good for beginners.
  • Grafted Dragon: Fire damage and grab attacks.
  • Bloodhound Claws: Bleed buildup, fast attacks.
  • Star Fist: Magic damage, late game.

Fist Build Tips

  • Learn the timing for grab attacks - they're devastating.
  • Use Bloodhound Step for mobility.
  • Position yourself for follow-up attacks.
  • Heavy armor reduces your mobility - avoid it.

Pros and Cons

Pros:

  • Unique and satisfying combat feel.
  • High mobility and combo potential.
  • Doesn't require specific weapons.
  • Great for roleplaying as a monk.

Cons:

  • Low range compared to weapons.
  • Requires precise positioning.
  • Some players find it gimmicky.
  • Weak against ranged enemies.
