Welcome to Elden Ring Builds

This website showcases all major build archetypes in Elden Ring. Whether you prefer raw melee power, swift strikes, magical spellcasting, or status effect mastery, find the build that suits your playstyle.

Strength Builds

Devastating melee damage with high survivability. Combines physical power with magic support.

  • Faith Strength - High Faith with supporting Strength
  • Strength Faith - Balanced hybrid combining melee and magic
  • Pure Strength - Maxed Strength for raw damage

Dexterity Builds

Swift attacks with superior mobility and critical damage. Various magical and status effect combinations.

  • Dex Intelligence - Swift spellcasters with sorceries
  • Dex Arcane - Status effect specialists with speed
  • Pure Dexterity - Maximum attack speed and critical damage

Intelligence Builds

Master of sorceries and magical damage. Access to glintstone magic and various spell combinations.

  • Int Dexterity - Intelligent duelists with quick strikes
  • Int Faith - Holy sorcerers with dual spell access
  • Pure Intelligence - Arcane sorcerers with maximum spell power

Arcane Builds

Status effect mastery and occult scaling. Specialize in poison, bleed, and unique occult weapons.

  • Arcane Dexterity - Swift status effect specialists
  • Arcane Faith - Holy incantations with occult power
  • Pure Arcane - Occult specialists with maximum status effects
